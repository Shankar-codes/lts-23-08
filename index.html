ABOUT US

LTS was started with an intention of providing a platform for academic research, pharma, food, calibration, instrumentation and testing domain as a whole to seek and offer services to mutual satisfaction. In this forum, the organizations can easily coordinate to extend and exchange their services to expedite their analytical journey to timely serve the customers.

The process is simple, user friendly, interactive and informative. The users can follow a simple step by step process to access the information required. A brief description of each service explained in a short will help the users to navigate through the website.
